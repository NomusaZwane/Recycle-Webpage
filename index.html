Why Recycling Matters

Recycling reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators, conserves natural resources, saves energy, reduces greenhouse gas emissions, and supports sustainable economic growth. Every item recycled contributes to a cleaner environment and a healthier planet for future generations.

Tips for effective recycling include properly sorting recyclable materials, cleaning containers before recycling, avoiding contamination by not including non-recyclable items, and reducing waste by reusing and repurposing whenever possible.
